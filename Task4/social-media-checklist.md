
>Do's 
  Use strong and secure passwords

  Always create passwords that include letters, numbers, and symbols to protect your accounts.
  
  Think before posting
  
  Make sure your posts are respectful and appropriate before sharing them online.
  
  Keep your personal information private
  
  Avoid sharing sensitive details such as phone numbers, addresses, or passwords.

> Don'ts 

  Do not share your passwords or OTPs

  Never give your login details to anyone, even friends.

  Do not accept requests from unknown people
  
  Avoid connecting with strangers who may have fake profiles.
  
  Do not post offensive or harmful content
