Stay Safe Online Checklist For College Students in India

Never share your One Time Password, UPI Personal Identification Number or banking passwords with anyone even if they claim to be from the bank.

Always verify payment requests before approving them in UPI apps like Google Pay, PhonePe or Paytm.

Do not approve requests.

Use passwords for all your accounts by combining letters, numbers and symbols.

Enable two factor authentication on your banking apps, email and social media accounts.

Avoid using Wi-Fi for financial transactions as hackers may access your data on unsecured networks.

This is a Financial and UPI Safety Tip.

Download apps from official stores like Google Play Store or Apple App Store to avoid malicious software.

Check website links carefully before entering payment details and avoid clicking on unknown links.

Regularly update your phone and apps to ensure security patches are installed.

Immediately report transactions, to your bank and block your account if you notice unusual activity.

This is a Financial and UPI Safety Tip.
